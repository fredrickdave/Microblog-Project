About Microblog

This Microblog website is a free platform for sharing short-form content, connecting with like-minded individuals and sharing your thoughts with your audience. This platform lets you follow and unfollow users you're interested in, which makes your home feed more personalized.

This project uses Flask framework to create a platform that is both lightweight and flexible, and more features will be added in the future to make the platform more engaging for everyone. Stay tuned!
